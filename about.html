Family-Run Used Appliance Shop

Capital Appliances is owned and operated by Vins and his wife Sarita. Together, they focus on bringing clean, reliable used appliances to local families, landlords, and small businesses.

Every piece is checked, tested, and fairly priced so you get the quality you need without big-box store markups.

What We Do

We specialize in fridges, stoves, washers, dryers, and dishwashers. Vins personally inspects each appliance, and Sarita helps make sure everything is clean, photographed, and clearly described.

Most of our listings come from local buy-and-sell and moving situations, so you get solid, gently used appliances that still have a lot of life left in them.

Need a specific size for a suite or condo? Let us know the measurements and we’ll help you find something that fits.
